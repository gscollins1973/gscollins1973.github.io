TRIPOD+AI statement: updated guidance for reporting clinical prediction models that use regression or machine learning methods

In 2019, we announced in a Lancet commentary> that we were going to embark on an update to the original 2015 version of the TRIPOD Statement to reflect the increasing interest in applying artificial intelligence to healthcare problems driven by advances in machine learning. A protocol describing our methodology was published in BMJ Open. In 2024, we published the TRIPOD+AI recommendations in the BMJ. The recommendations now supersede the original 2015 recommendations.
  • TRIPOD+AI: the checklist paper
