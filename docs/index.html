Welcome!

I am a 125th Anniversary Chair and Professor of Medical Statistics in the Department of Applied Health Sciences at the University of Birmingham. In 2024, I was awarded a NIHR Senior Investigator award. I previously worked at the University of Oxford for 19 years, where I was a Professor of Medical Statistics and Director of the Centre for Statistics in Medicine.

My research interests include

  • methodology for clinical prediction models (inc. predictive AI/machine learning for healthcare)

  • methodology for evaluation and fairness assessment of clinical prediction models

  • reporting standards and guidelines

  • research transparency

  • meta-science

  • open science
